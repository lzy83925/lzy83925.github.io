kubernetes里如何彻底删除一个容器

October 31, 2017 04:10 AM

比如一开始我部署的kubernetes dashboard部署的有点问题，我现在想把这个彻底删除，而且是级联删除，通过yaml文件来彻底删除
kubectl delete -f https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kubernetes/dashboard/master/src/deploy/recommended/kubernetes-dashboard.yaml
可以看到如下输出
secret "kubernetes-dashboard-certs" deleted serviceaccount "kubernetes-dashboard" deleted role "kubernetes-dashboard-minimal" deleted rolebinding "kubernetes-dashboard-minimal" deleted deployment "kubernetes-dashboard" deleted service "kubernetes-dashboard" deleted

但是我们发现Pods并没有删除,执行下面命令
kubectl delete --all pods --namespace=kube-system

如果有必要，继续执行下面命令
kubectl delete deployment kubernetes-dashboard --namespace=kube-system
